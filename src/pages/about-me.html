About Me

Profile

I’m Kristine de Jesus, a 22-year-old front-end developer based in the Philippines. I first knew about front-end development during my last semester in 1st year of college as a BS-IT student, but I never really had a plan to pursue it. However, thanks to K-pop boy group SEVENTEEN’s official non-responsive website, I got curious about developing a website, thus, here I am.

I self-studied most of my knowledge in front-end development with the useful resources found on the internet such as mdn web docs, The Odin Project, 4 freecodecamp, and YouTube tutorials. I also enrolled in Dr. Angela Yu’s Web Development Bootcamp which helped me get started on front-end development.
